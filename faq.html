Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I participate?
A. It's easy! You can participate by sending in your entries/answers to qsr.online.event@gmail.com.

Q. What if I can't send my entries via email? Is there any way else to send my entries?
A. You can put up your entries on the Sketch Wall on the ground floor foyer (not applicable for participants from outside the college. They have send their entries via email only.).

Q. It's so easy to cheat in this event!
A. Yes, it is very easy to cheat in this event, but we would prefer that you play the entire event honestly. It'll make you as well as us very happy! :)

Q. Who is judging?
A. We will get some faculty members to judge for further levels as soon as possible.

Q. Why is this event before QSR7 starts?
A. This event is supposed to be very laidback so that anyone who doesn't wanna go out can play it!

Q. How long is this event going to be?
A. A week.
