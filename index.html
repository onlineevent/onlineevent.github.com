About the Online Event

The Online Event is QSR7's most chilled out event! Your work is just to enjoy it whether you are sitting in front of a computer, on a laptop, using your phone or your tablet PC!

Isn't it cool?

The event is simple: there are only going to be 3 levels in total. All the participants will be able to play for the first level, the qualified participants from the first level would be promoted to the second level and the qualified participants from the second level would be promoted to the third and the final level.

There is only going to be one winner.

The brief regarding all the levels would be put up as soon as the previous level is over.
